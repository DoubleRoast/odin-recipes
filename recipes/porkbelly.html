Roasted Pork Belly

Description

This will be the recipe of a roasted pork belly

The next part will be the ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1kg Pork belly with skin on
  • 1 1/2 tsp Olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp fennel powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Dry skin overnight
  2. Preheat oven
  3. Season flesh
  4. Wrap in foil
  5. Season skin
  6. Slow roast 2 1/2 hours
  7. Increase heat
  8. Level pork
  9. 30 minutes on high hear
  10. Rest 10 minutes
  11. Serve
